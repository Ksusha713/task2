7 ways to find happiness in Finland

  • Finnish happiness is calm and peaceful
  • Relax in safety
  • Witness the magic of the Northern Lights
  • Stay energized in the Midnight Sun
  • Sweat out the negative in a sauna
  • Forage wild food and taste happiness
  • Reconnect with nature
  • Talk to the locals

7 Best things to do in Finland

  1. Stroll around an old wooden town
  2. Explore a lighthouse island
  3. Hike in one of Finland's 41 national parks
  4. See iconic Nordic architecture and design
  5. Meet the real Santa Claus
  6. Enjoy unique accommodation
  7. Go downhill or cross-country skiing or snowboarding

Must-visit quirky saunas in Finland

  1. Hover over Helsinki in a sauna on a Ferris wheel

    2. This sauna in a Ferris wheel gondola offers a thrilling ride above the Helsinki harbour.

  2. Discover hot and cold in ice and snow saunas
  3. Find your peace in the Sauna Temple

    4. The Sauna Temple invites you to find your inner zen enjoying a unique silent bathing and full-bodied sauna experience in a traditional yurt.

  4. Float across Finnish waters

    5. Enjoy a relaxing steam bath and then dip right into the water, all while soaking in the natural beauty around you.

  5. Glide up a fell in a sauna gondola
  6. Experience sauna history in a true sauna village

Must-visit wild food restaurants in Finland

  • Restaurant Grön, Helsinki – Urban restaurant with a farm

    • Grön provides seasonal menus that reflect the natural flow of nature.

  • Restaurant Nokka, Helsinki – One of Finland's best with a sustainability focus

    • Chef and owner Ari Ruoho harvests, hunts, and fishes to secure the best ingredients for the restaurant.

  • Restaurant Natura, Helsinki – Modern and innovative cuisine in the heart of the

    • Natura focuses on Finnish game, vegetables, and sustainable seafood.

  • Restaurant VÅR, Porvoo – Playful classics in a historical setting
  • Restaurant Nili, Rovaniemi – Modern cuisine in a rustic Lapland setting
